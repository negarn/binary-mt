Financial Account

Authenticate your Binary.com account to create an MT5 Financial Account.

Kindly email the following documents to support@binary.com to authenticate your account:

  • A scanned copy of your passport, driving licence (provisional or full), or identity card that shows your full name and date of birth.
  • A scanned copy of a utility bill or bank statement that is no more than three months old.

To deposit or withdraw from your MT5 Account, switch to your Binary.com Real Account.
